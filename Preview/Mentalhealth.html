Understanding Mental Health & COVID-19 Fast Facts

  • Anxiety reported at rates 3x higher than 2019
  • Depression reported at rates 4x higher than 2019
  • 1 in 10 people will start or increase their substance abuse

My Self-Care and Dealing with Anxiety

  • Developed healthier habits + eat foods that make me feel good
  • Focused on the things I could control in my own life
  • Cut out toxic people/activities
  • Limit time on social media
  • Know and accept I won't feel 100% everyday (it's okay to have bad days)
  • Try to push myself out of my comfort zone even if it's something small
  • PUT MYSELF FIRST!
